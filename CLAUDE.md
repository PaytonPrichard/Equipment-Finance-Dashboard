# ABL Deal Screening Platform

## Writing Style
- No em dashes in user-facing copy
- Short sentences. Direct language. No filler.
- Sound like a credit professional, not a marketer
- Avoid words: leverage (as verb), streamline, empower, robust, seamless, utilize
- Prefer periods over semicolons. Commas over dashes.
- Keep UI text scannable: 10 words max for labels, 20 words max for descriptions

## UI/Design Principles
- White space is a feature, not wasted space
- Every screen should have ONE clear primary action
- Reduce visible options. Hide secondary actions behind menus or collapsed sections
- Information hierarchy: primary (white/gold), secondary (slate-400), tertiary (slate-600)
- Glass-card dark theme with gold (#D4A843) accents on charcoal (#141210)
